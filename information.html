Gifts

We set up home together 11 years ago so while we appreciate you might want to buy us a gift, we have what we need (lucky us) so please don’t! We just want you to enjoy our special day as much as possible, so if the money you’d have spent on something for us is burning a hole in your pocket, use it towards paying the babysitter to stay that bit longer or a taxi to get home safely.

And if none of that applies and you still want to do something, we’ve chosen Single Homeless Project (SHP) as our preferred charity, should you wish to make a donation. SHP is a London-wide charity working to prevent homelessness and help vulnerable and socially excluded people to transform their lives. If we can help them together in some small way that would be amazing. But please, don’t feel compelled to donate, the main thing is we get to see you all!

Dress Code

You know us—we like to make an effort but also stay chilled and we want everyone to feel relaxed, whatever they’re wearing. We’re likely to be sporting sharp suits but avoiding ties.
